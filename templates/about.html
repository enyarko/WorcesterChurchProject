About Worcester Church of Christ

A Bible-based church committed to worship, fellowship, service, and spiritual growth.

Who We Are

Worcester Church of Christ is a congregation of Christians seeking to follow the teachings of Jesus Christ and the pattern of the New Testament.

We are committed to worshiping God, studying Scripture, encouraging one another, serving our community, and helping people grow in faith.

Our Mission

To glorify God by teaching the gospel, strengthening believers, serving others, and helping souls grow in Christ.

What We Value

Our faith and practice are shaped by Scripture.

Biblical Teaching

We seek to teach and follow God’s Word with humility, clarity, and conviction.

Christ-Centered Worship

Our worship is focused on honoring God and remembering the sacrifice of Christ.

Loving Fellowship

We encourage one another as members of God’s family and servants of Christ.
